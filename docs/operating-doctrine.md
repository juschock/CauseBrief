# Snickerdoodle Operating Doctrine

Parent company: **Racoben Engineering, LLC**

Product: **Snickerdoodle** — customer-facing brand only. Do not rename the LLC.

## What we sell

We do **not** sell:

- marketing
- advertising
- copywriting
- AI
- templates

We sell:

```text
Campaign Execution Packages
```

Customer experience:

```text
One survey

↓

Complete campaign execution package
```

Internal production:

```text
Survey

↓

Campaign classification

↓

Fact ledger

↓

Campaign strategy

↓

AI-assisted production

↓

AI critique

↓

AI polish

↓

Deterministic QA

↓

Rachel review

↓

Delivery
```

## Core operating rule

Every Snickerdoodle package exists to move **one clearly defined audience** toward **one clearly defined action**.

Not marketing. Not content. Not AI. **Campaign execution.**

## Positioning

> Snickerdoodle prepares complete, human-reviewed campaign execution packages for small organizations.

Primary audiences:

- nonprofits
- community organizations
- local businesses
- service businesses
- event organizers

We are not nonprofit-only. We are also not broad marketing. The abstraction is **campaign execution**.

## Fact ledger

Mandatory. No campaign asset may introduce dates, sponsors, statistics, prices, links, donation matches, reviews, testimonials, or impact claims unless those facts exist in the ledger.

## AI and review

- AI is **internal only** — never customer-facing
- Rachel remains the **final quality gate**
- Human review is required before every delivery

## Public product constraints (V1)

Preserve:

- current UI (v0 palette, typography, imagery, section layout)
- App Router
- no customer-facing AI
- no auth, DB, or payment
- standalone product repo
- Racoben parent architecture at `racoben.com/snickerdoodle`

## Internal north star

```text
We do not sell marketing.

We do not sell copy.

We do not sell AI.

We sell campaign execution packages.

Every package exists to move one clearly defined audience toward one clearly defined action through a coordinated sequence of human-reviewed campaign assets.
```
