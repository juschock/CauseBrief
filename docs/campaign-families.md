# Snickerdoodle Campaign Families

Three launch families. Everything else flows from these.

## 1. Cause / Nonprofit Campaigns

Examples:

- fundraising
- year-end
- Giving Tuesday
- adoption events
- volunteer drives
- community events

Primary actions:

```text
donate · attend · volunteer · register · share · sponsor · follow up
```

Public sample: **Animal Rescue Adoption Event**, **Nonprofit Year-End Appeal**

---

## 2. Local Customer Acquisition

Examples:

- grand opening
- seasonal promotion
- first-time customer
- review campaign
- referral campaign
- quote campaign

Primary actions:

```text
visit · buy · order · book · call · redeem · review · refer
```

---

## 3. Booking / Reservation Campaigns

Examples:

- grooming
- salons
- restaurants
- consultations
- estimates
- classes

Primary actions:

```text
book · reserve · schedule · request estimate · join class
```

Public sample: **Pet Groomer Holiday Booking Campaign**

---

## Intake routing

Early survey questions should determine:

1. organization type
2. campaign family
3. desired action

See `lib/intake.ts` for form fields.
