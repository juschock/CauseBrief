# Human QA checklist (before delivery)

- [ ] Event date, time, and location/link are correct
- [ ] CTA is clear and matches the brief
- [ ] Audience language is consistent throughout
- [ ] Tone matches customer request
- [ ] No hallucinated facts, sponsors, or partners
- [ ] No fake claims or unsupported urgency
- [ ] No misleading donation or sales language
- [ ] All requested channels are included
- [ ] Copy is usable without extra explanation
- [ ] Emails have strong subject lines
- [ ] Social posts are appropriate length per platform
- [ ] Phrases to include/avoid were respected
- [ ] File names and folder structure are clean
- [ ] README_How_to_Use.txt is included
